Visualization Description

This visualization shows data based on different seasons from 2012 to 2015.

By clicking on one line in the form, it shows the variation tendency of this selected type. You can click several types to show them in the figure together.

To remove the selected type, you just click on this type again.

The two figures below shows the temperature and humidity of Seattle in 2015, which is related to the season feature. For example, summer has high temperature and high humidity.
